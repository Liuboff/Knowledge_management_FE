The Knowledge Management Information System (KMIS) is a comprehensive software solution designed to facilitate the effective management and dissemination of knowledge within an organization. The system aims to streamline the processes of capturing, storing, sharing, and utilizing knowledge to enhance organizational learning, decision-making, and innovation.

Project duration: {{project.dateStart| date:'dd.MM.yyyy'}} - {{project.dateEnd| date:'dd.MM.yyyy'}}

Objective

Create a unified platform for storing and managing various types of knowledge assets, including documents, manuals, guidelines, and best practices. Ensure that knowledge is easily accessible to all authorized users, irrespective of their location or device. Implement advanced search and retrieval mechanisms to quickly locate relevant information.
